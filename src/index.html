John Fleming

Full Stack Developer

Hi! I'm John.

I'm an aspiring developer currently in the full stack program at Nashville Software School. In my day job, I manage a client success team at marketing company in the tech industry, but I'm making the leap to software engineering so I can do more of the hands on work creating awesome user interfaces and solving problems through technology.

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work around other software engineers in a variety of settings. I've held roles in healthcare project management related to website design and natural language processing initiatives through machine learning, as well as a sales role at a custom software development agency. I started to get more hands on experience with technology when I assumed product ownership responsibility as the Volunteer Manager and Salesforce Administrator at Habitat for Humanity of Greater Nashville.

With each opportunity and now in my current role within client success, I got to see first hand just how valuable the software engineers were in building creative solutions to challenges my teams experienced. I wanted to be a part of that solution and after 6 months of self-study, I was hooked! I decided to officially apply to Nashville Software School to help me stay focused on my goal.
